### 2. Why did EOD fail?

**eod_failure.json** from droplet:
```json
{
  "date": "2026-02-13",
  "exit_code": 1,
  "stderr": "WARNING EOD missing or incomplete — re-running for 2026-02-13.\nINFO Generated ... wheel_daily_review_2026-02-13.md\n...\nERROR missed_money_numeric.all_numeric is False; critical logs missing. EOD FAIL.\nEOD pipeline failed with exit code 1\n"
```

**Cause:** `board/eod/root_cause` writes `missed_money_numeric.json` with `all_numeric: false` when required logs (blocked_trades.jsonl, exit_attribution.jsonl, etc.) are incomplete or missing fields. The EOD runner treats this as a hard failure unless `--allow-missing-missed-money` is passed.

### 3. Crontab and schedule

- **Schedule:** `30 21 * * 1-5` (21:30 UTC Mon–Fri)
- **Script:** `board/eod/eod_confirmation.py` (which calls `run_stock_quant_officer_eod.py` via `run_full_eod`)
- **Log:** `/root/stock-bot/logs/cron_eod.log` (and `/var/log/eod_confirmation.log` depending on crontab variant)

### 4. Fix implemented

1. **`cron_diagnose_and_fix.py`**  
   - Added retry: when first `force_run_eod` fails and stderr contains `missed_money_numeric`, retry with `--allow-missing-missed-money`.
   - `force_run_eod` now accepts `allow_missing_missed_money` and passes it to `eod_confirmation.py --allow-missing-missed-money`.

2. **Commit:** `cron_diagnose_and_fix: retry EOD with --allow-missing-missed-money when missed_money_numeric fails (RCA 2/13)`
